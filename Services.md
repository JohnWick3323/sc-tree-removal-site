---

### Dead Tree Removal

A dead tree might look harmless standing in your yard — but it is one of the most dangerous hazards on any property.

Dead trees lose structural integrity fast. Without warning, they can collapse onto your home, your fence, your vehicle, or worse — a family member.

South Carolina's heat and humidity accelerate decay in trees like Water Oak and Sweetgum, making dead tree removal a common and urgent need across the state.

Our team identifies the safest removal method for every dead tree situation and executes the job quickly — leaving your yard clean and your property protected.

---

### Hazardous Tree Removal

A hazardous tree does not have to be dead to be dangerous.

Leaning trees, storm-split trunks, root-damaged trees, and trees growing too close to power lines or your home's foundation all qualify as serious hazards that need immediate attention.

South Carolina's hurricane season and severe summer thunderstorms regularly push already-compromised trees to their breaking point.

Our experienced crews assess every hazardous tree situation carefully and execute a safe, controlled removal — no matter how complex the job.

---

### Crane-Assisted Tree Removal

Some trees simply cannot be felled the traditional way — whether due to size, location, or the risk of damage to surrounding structures.

Crane-assisted tree removal is the safest solution for large trees in tight spaces, trees overhanging rooftops, or removals near power lines and fences.

South Carolina Tree Removal Experts operates heavy-lift crane equipment handled by experienced operators who have performed this specialized work for decades.

This method allows us to lift and remove entire trees — or large sections — with precision, keeping your property fully protected throughout the process.

---

### Tree Cutting & Felling

Tree cutting and felling is the controlled process of bringing a tree down safely in a planned direction.

It requires a thorough site assessment, knowledge of the tree's weight distribution, and a clear understanding of surrounding structures and hazards.

Our crews have performed thousands of controlled tree felling jobs across South Carolina — from residential backyards in Lexington to large rural properties in Anderson and Aiken.

Every felling job includes full debris cleanup so your property is left exactly as we found it — minus the tree.

### Forestry Mulching

Forestry mulching is one of the most efficient and environmentally responsible land clearing methods available today.

Using specialized mulching equipment, we grind trees, brush, and vegetation on-site — turning the cleared material into a natural ground cover that protects the soil.

This single-pass process eliminates the need for burning or hauling, saving time and significantly reducing clearing costs.

Forestry mulching is ideal for residential lots, rural acreage, pipeline corridors, and large development sites across South Carolina.

---
