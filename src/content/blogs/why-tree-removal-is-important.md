When hurricane season approaches South Carolina, homeowners quickly rush to secure their windows, stock up on supplies, and prepare their families. However, one of the most critical elements of storm preparation is often overlooked: **your trees**.

Large, mature trees add immense value and beauty to your property, but they can also become significant liabilities when facing wind speeds exceeding 70 mph.

## The Hidden Dangers of Unhealthy Trees

Many trees look perfectly healthy on the outside while rotting from within. Our certified arborists frequently discover:

1. **Root Rot**: Compromised root systems that cannot hold the tree's weight in saturated soil.
2. **Hollow Trunks**: Structural weaknesses that make the tree susceptible to snapping.
3. **Dead Branches**: "Widow-makers" that can easily break off and become airborne projectiles.

## Why DIY Tree Removal is Never the Answer

It might be tempting to grab a chainsaw and tackle that leaning pine tree yourself, but DIY tree removal is extremely dangerous. 

Professional tree removal experts bring:
- **Specialized Equipment**: Cranes, bucket trucks, and rigging gear to dismantle trees safely.
- **Expertise & Training**: Knowledge of physics and tree anatomy to predict exactly how and where branches will fall.
- **Insurance Protection**: Full liability and worker's compensation coverage to protect you from catastrophic financial loss if something goes wrong.

## When to Call an Arborist

Don't wait until the storm warnings are on TV. Schedule a professional assessment if you notice:
- Mushrooms growing at the base of the trunk
- Deep cracks or missing bark
- The tree suddenly leaning
- Dead branches in the upper canopy
