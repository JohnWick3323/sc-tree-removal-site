## 3. "Frequently Asked Questions" (SEO Boost + Schema)
**Location:** Above final CTA / Footer
**Design:** Dark theme (`bg-neutral-900`) accordion or grid.

* **Q: Do I need a permit to remove a tree in South Carolina?**
  * *A:* Permit requirements vary by municipality. For example, Charleston strictly protects "Grand Trees" (24+ inches DBH), while Greenville and Columbia have their own landscape ordinances. We handle all local permit applications to ensure your tree removal is 100% legal and compliant.
* **Q: How can I tell if my oak or pine tree is diseased?**
  * *A:* Common SC tree issues like Oak Wilt, Oak Leaf Blister, or Southern Pine Beetle infestations often present as thinning canopies, early leaf drop, or sawdust at the trunk base. If you notice these signs, contact us for a professional health assessment.
* **Q: Does homeowners insurance cover tree removal?**
  * *A:* If a tree falls on a covered structure (like your house or fence) due to a storm, your insurance typically covers the removal cost. We work directly with local SC insurance adjusters to streamline your claim process.
* **Q: What do you do with the wood and stump?**
  * *A:* We offer comprehensive cleanup. We can haul all debris away, chip the branches into landscape mulch, or leave the wood cut to firewood length. We also provide professional stump grinding so you can replant or landscape the area immediately.
