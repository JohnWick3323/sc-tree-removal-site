A hazardous tree does not have to be dead to be dangerous.

Leaning trees, storm-split trunks, root-damaged trees, and trees growing too close to power lines or your home's foundation all qualify as serious hazards that need immediate attention.

South Carolina's hurricane season and severe summer thunderstorms regularly push already-compromised trees to their breaking point.

Our experienced crews assess every hazardous tree situation carefully and execute a safe, controlled removal — no matter how complex the job.

South Carolina Tree Removal Experts operates heavy-lift crane equipment handled by experienced operators who have performed this specialized work for decades.

This method allows us to lift and remove entire trees — or large sections — with precision, keeping your property fully protected throughout the process.

Our crews have performed thousands of controlled tree felling jobs across South Carolina — from residential backyards in Lexington to large rural properties in Anderson and Aiken.
